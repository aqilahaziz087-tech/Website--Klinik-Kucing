About Our Clinic

DQA's Paws Cat Clinic is dedicated to providing compassionate and high-quality veterinary care for every feline friend. Our mission is to ensure your furry companion stays healthy, happy, and comfortable throughout their lives.


Meet Our Team

Our team consists of experienced veterinarians and trained veterinary nurses who all share a deep love for cats. We are committed to creating a friendly and supportive environment for both our patients and their owners.

  • Dr.Aiman

    Head Veterinarian
    Dr. Aiman founded DQA's Paws with a vision to create a clinic that focuses solely on the unique needs of cats. He has over 15 years of experience in feline medicine.

  • Aishah

    Senior Vet Nurse
